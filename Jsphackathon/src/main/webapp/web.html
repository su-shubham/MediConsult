FAQ

Are the specialists available 24/7?

Our specialists are available for consultation from 9AM to 9PM on all the 7 days of the week

Payment not reflected in TDH App?

It may take a while for the payment to reflect in the app. If there is no progress, reach out to our customer care team.

What are the qualifications of the specialist?

All specialists on the Tata Health platform are highly qualified and experienced physicians with a minimum qualification of M.D. (Doctor of Medicine) in their chosen field of specialisation, in addition to their MBBS degree.

Lost internet connectivity mid chat. What happens to my payment?

Yes you can continue the chat. We will help you connect to a specialist without any extra cost.

Payment Failure | Repeated Payment Failure

Please re-initiate the payment in 10 minutes. In case of repeated failures, you can reach out to customer care and they will assist you.

What is your refund policy ?

In case you are not satisfied with the consultation for any reason, you can reach out to our customer care with a request for a refund. Our customer care team will process the refund after verifying your case details.
